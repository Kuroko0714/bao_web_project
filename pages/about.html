Q: Who are we?
A: We are dessert club. A group of Tanghin dessert lovers who wish to have a good mood every day.
Q: What will we do?
A: We will hold many activities to our members to learn about desserts. For example, how to make a dessert and the stories of desserts. Desserts can make you feel glad and relaxed.
We wish:
We hope everyone won’t usually feel blue. We believe that desserts can sweep away all your bad feelings.
Q: How to and who can join us?
A: Everyone can join us in any time! You just need to use one of your fingers to fill in the form. Then, you are our member. Come and join us immediately!
You can learn and make creative desserts here. Toothsome desserts can sweep away people's worries.
Don’t hesitate, come and join us!
